# Projects

## Aerial wildlife monitoring

Developed deep learning solutions for dense animal detection in aerial imagery, including new attention modules and multi-species counting strategies. This work is designed for ecological monitoring, conservation planning, and remote sensing applications.

## Visual odometry for AGVs

Designed and implemented a visual odometry system for autonomous guided vehicles, using camera-based motion estimation to support precise indoor and outdoor navigation.

## Applied AI systems

Built prototypes for robotics and sensor fusion, combining academic research with practical implementation. These projects emphasize reproducibility, code clarity, and performance evaluation.

## Future directions

I am interested in continuing work at the intersection of computer vision, robotics, and environmental sensing, with a focus on resilient, deployable systems for real-world research challenges.
