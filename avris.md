## Avris Research Projects During Bachelor of Science

This page documents applied research and development work carried out during the Bachelor of Science in Mechatronics in collaboration with the **Avris** research group. These projects span areas including robotics, computer vision, and sensor integration, and represent hands-on work alongside academic formation.

### SIGTROV GT: Autonomous Guided Vehicle Design and Prototyping

**Area:** Robotics / Mechanical Design / Autonomous Systems

**Description:**
SIGTROV GT is a holonomic Autonomous Guided Vehicle (AGV) designed and physically prototyped as part of applied research activities within the Avris group. The platform features omnidirectional mecanum wheels enabling full freedom of motion in planar environments, and was conceived for industrial navigation and research applications. The design process spanned from initial concept and 3D CAD modelling through to structural fabrication and hardware integration, in collaboration with EIA University and AMCO.

**Keywords:** AGV, holonomic platform, mecanum wheels, CAD design, robotics, prototype
